Biography

Gongyao Jiang is a first-year Ph.D. student at HKUST (GZ Campus), supervised by Prof. Qiong Luo. Before that, he have earned the master’s degree at Tianjin University, supervised by Prof. Meishan Zhang. His research interest is data science and natural language processing.
